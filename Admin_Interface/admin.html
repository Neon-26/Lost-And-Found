About

PATEROS TECHNOLOGICAL COLLEGE

Lost and Found Systems designed to help our school community to locate and to return the misplaced belongings. As students, we understand how stressful it is to lose an important items like ID's, notebooks, gadgets and other items that might lose when you are in school. So we built a simple platform that anyone/students can reports lost items or post if you found items, and connect with the rightful owner quickly.

This project was developed by BSIT students who wanted to apply their skills on System Analysis and Design and solve campus problems from losing important items. Our goal is to make the lost-and-found process more effiecient and organized, reducing the need for announcements, group chats, and manual searching. Through the webiste, we hope to encourage responsibility and honesty, also the cooperation of students, while making our school more connected.
